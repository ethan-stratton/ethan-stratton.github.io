THE MARK

"To miss the mark is not always to aim wrong."

Your community is all that you know. Before you were born, the cities fell. The roads broke. Nobody alive remembers why.

All that's left is river valleys, forests, and the people stubborn enough to rebuild. Settlements are walls of rough-cut stone, fields carved from forest floor, with survival measured in harvests.

A confederation of unified settlements originated in the north. It began as mutual defense. Now, settlements that don't join voluntarily tend to join anyway.

Between the settlements are wandering Ash Walkers — displaced, desperate, and often dangerous. Some are families with nowhere to go. Some are raiders.

Brannhaven was the old name of your settlement — the name has stuck. Two hundred souls behind a wall older than anyone alive.

After 30 years, the old leader croaked. You were next in line.
