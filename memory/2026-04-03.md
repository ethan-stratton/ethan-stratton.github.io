## Combat System Overhaul (01:05–01:40 UTC)

### Design Decisions
- **Stagger/Break system implemented** — FF7R/Doom Eternal inspired loop: basic attacks build stagger → BREAK state (2.5s) → 3× damage + ether drops → spend ether on fusions
- **Dual combos always unlocked** — removed Tome of Binding requirement from `RefreshCombo()`
- **Silk essence removed from demo** — only Cinder, Pulse, Bone available (Room 6 crystals)
- **Shrine of the Lady** — only gives Nimue's Tear now (no more Tome of Binding)
- **Ether economy**: passive regen (already 12 EP/sec in code), +3 EP per hit on Broken enemies, +2 EP per kill
- **Element counters**: weakness = 2× stagger buildup (not damage). Cinder→nature, Pulse→bone/undead, Bone→fire
- **Status effects**: Cinder=Ignite DoT+stagger ticks, Pulse=burst stagger spike (+15), Bone=Fracture (3 stacks, +50% stagger)
- **Combo presets**: scroll wheel cycles presets, keys 4-9 for direct select (1/2/3 stay as component cycling)
- **Stagger threshold**: lowered from `hp × 0.6` to `hp × 0.35` after testing showed enemies never reached Break

## Game Design Videos Analyzed (02:24–02:31 UTC)

### Adam Millard — "The Art of Novelty" (Tactical Breach Wizards)
- Content ≠ Novelty — must make player THINK differently, not just see different things
- Fewer meaningful choices > stat bumps; explore ideas deeply (introduce→test→twist→finale)
- Synergistic mechanics = emergent novelty (Balatro); force comfort zone exits via weaknesses (Doom Eternal)
- Risk for us: 343 combos where 300 feel identical

### "What Makes Combat Feel Playful" (God of War / Doom / DMC5)
- Suck-to-target: generous hit detection on near-misses
- Strike assist: knockback toward where player is LOOKING, not just away
- Per-enemy stun > global resource (validates our stagger/break system)
- No stamina/no hoarding: passive ether regen is correct design
- DMC5: reverse-engineer emotions; each enemy needs one-sentence behavioral identity

### Proposed improvements:
1. Knockback toward cursor (not away from projectile)
2. "Ether full" nag pulse
3. Enemy behavioral identity (one-sentence roles)
4. First-combo-discovery freeze frame
5. Generous bolt hit detection (near-miss grazing)
