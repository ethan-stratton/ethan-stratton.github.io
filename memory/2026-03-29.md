### Critical Feedback from Ethan (v0.2 playtest):
- **"Lacking FUN"** — the system is too mathematical, not spectacle-driven
- Burst should be shotgun, not 8-way nova
- Arc useless in top-down (no gravity relevance)
- Essences are weak — need visual transformation, not just tint+status
- Movement not dynamic enough
- Wants: familiars, bone constructs, lightning birds, explosions, buzzsaws, lasers, carpet bombs, melee attachments, player trails, auras, jumping
- Wants roguelite elements: relics (Risk of Rain/Isaac style), between-wave shop
- Inspirations: diep.io gun variety, Vampire Survivors, Binding of Isaac, Mega Man
- Bone feels weakest essence

### Design Pivot (agreed direction):
- Flip from bottom-up (orthogonal math) to top-down (spectacle first)
- Essences become visual transformations: Cinder=EXPLOSIONS, Bone=SUMMONS, Sap=OVERGROWTH, Hollow=BLACK HOLES, Pulse=LIGHTNING, Silk=WEBS, Brine=ACID PUDDLES
- New Forms planned: Beam (continuous laser), Saw (boomerang buzzsaw), Barrage (carpet bomb), Lance (short-range stab), Familiar (creature companion)
- Fourth axis: Relics (passive run modifiers, Arthurian themed)
- v0.3 plan: shotgun, lance, beam, Cinder explosions, Pulse lightning, player trails, better dash feel
