## Rat Park v6 — THE ISOLATION PHASE (major structural change)
- Game restructured into 3 acts:
  - Act 1 (Days 1-2): Rat Park — build bonds
  - Act 2 (Day 3): THE HAND takes you → isolation cage
  - Act 3 (Day 4): Rescue/resolution based on bonds built
- dayLength increased to 120s, maxDays to 4
- "The Hand" sequence: dramatic dialog, NPC reactions (Pip screams, Hazel calls, Bruno silent), fade to black
- Isolation cage: 160px wide (vs 640 park), dark metal, solid walls, only food dish + drug bottle
- Drug bottle glows MORE intensely in isolation (only color in the room)
- Meters drain 1.5x faster, activity has NO way to refill
- Inner monologue every 20s: "It's so quiet." / "Was Pip real?" / "Nobody's coming."
- Echo particles if bonds were strong: faint friend voices
- Rescue progress based on bondSnapshot (social + totalFriendship) — strong bonds = rescue in ~60s
- Rescue: scratching sounds, closest friend finds you, reunion in park
- Reunion dialogs differ based on whether you drank in isolation
- New endings: CONNECTED / SURVIVED / RECOVERED / DEPENDENT / ISOLATED
- Ethan's key insight: "what if YOU get taken away" — the stress isn't abstract events, it's personal removal from everything you built. Friends rescuing you is the payoff.
