### Full Wilbur-Pipeline World Map Rewrite (commit `980cf91`)
- Map changed to 60×100 (vertical continent)
- 35 tile types (up from ~17): DeepOcean, ShallowOcean, Reef, Beach, RockyShore, Grassland, Savanna, Jungle, Hills, Foothills, HighMountain, Glacier, Taiga, SnowForest, FrozenLake, Dunes, Wasteland, Marsh, Floodplain, River, Lake, Cave, Ruins, Oasis + originals
- 15-stage generation pipeline:
  1. Fractal noise (4 layers: height 7oct, moisture 5oct, temp 3oct, ridge 5oct)
  2. Gaussian island shaping (Wilbur formula: curval×exp(-sqr(r/k))-sqr(r))
  3. Mountain ridge injection
  4. Basin filling (Planchon-Darboux) — 2 passes with noise between
  5. Water flux accumulation (sort descending, cascade rainfall)
  6. Incise flow erosion (slope × sqrt(flux) × 0.015, capped)
  7. Thermal erosion (4 passes)
  8. Coastline smoothing (3-pass majority-neighbor filter)
  9. Biome assignment (height × temp × moisture, 6 elevation bands)
  10. Rivers from flux network (threshold 25+)
  11. Lakes at local minima with high flux
  12. Desert detail (dunes from ridge noise, oases near water)
  13. Caves at mountain bases
  14. Ruins in dense forest/jungle/wasteland
  15. Volcano (one per map, hot zone high mountains)
- Seed 42, deterministic. Plan: iterate until map looks good, freeze it, eventually remove generation code.

## Worldbuilding Framework (from "Seven Levels of Worldbuilding" video discussion)

Ethan asked to remember this deeply. The framework for Genesis worldbuilding:

**Target: Level 5 with selective Level 6.**

### The Five-Point Plan (Ethan said "this is important"):
1. **Start with the creation myth** — write it as conflicting accounts (faction A says X, faction B says Y). Gives Level 5 for free.
2. **Pick ONE core power/technology constraint** (Level 4) — the "no thinking machines" of our world. One rule that cascades into combat, society, architecture.
3. **Build the map AFTER the history** (Level 1) — don't draw land then justify it. Know what happened, let geography reflect it.
4. **Let Rule of Cool live inside constraints** (Level 2→4/5) — one sentence of justification tied to history/power rules turns cool ideas into deep worldbuilding.
5. **Surface identity comes from gameplay** (Level 3) — each region should feel different to PLAY, not just look different. Visual identity follows mechanical identity.

### Key Principle:
"Write the myth first, derive everything else from it." Genesis source material already has the deep history and cultural weight baked in.
