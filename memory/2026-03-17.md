### Enemy AI Research
- Compiled research from Reddit threads + Terraria wiki knowledge into game-design/research/2026-03-17-enemy-ai-research.md
- Terraria AI types: Fighter (walker), Slime (hopper), Demon Eye (flyer), Worm (multi-segment), Caster (teleport+shoot), Passive-until-aggro, Mimic
- Key insight: simple AI + great encounter design beats complex AI. Dark Souls enemies are predictable = best combat. Terraria enemies are numbered AI types = incredible game.
- F.E.A.R.: GOAP system, but audio callouts ("He's flanking!") sold the illusion of intelligence more than the actual AI
- Halo: enemy species have behavioral PERSONALITY (Grunts panic, Elites coordinate)
- Alien Isolation: Xenomorph learns from encounters you survive, not deaths
- Next enemy to build: Hopper (slime-type, hop toward player with rhythm)
- Design rule: each enemy has ONE clear pattern, personality through movement
