## Session ~16:15-16:32 UTC — Bug Fixes + Suit System + Creature Anti-Spaz

### Suit Integrity System
- **Removed auto-regen**: Suit was healing to next 25% mark at 1.5%/s in "neutral" zones — completely negated all degradation. Now permanent resource.
- **Water submersion**: 0.15%/s when chest-deep. Below 10% integrity, water shorts battery too.
- **Ionic storms** (new weather): `IsIonicStorm => StormEnergy > 0.8f && IsStorming && Temperature > 0.55f`. Degrades suit 0.4%/s BUT charges battery 2%/s. EVE comments on it.
- **Heavy hits**: 3+ damage = 0.5% suit loss per point above 2.
- **Storm wear**: Regular storms 0.05%/s.
- **EVE alerts** at 25%, 15%, 5% integrity thresholds.
- Commit `90a0b9b`

### Creature Direction Cooldown (Anti-Spaz)

- Wall collisions use `force: true` to bypass cooldown (physical constraint).
- Applied to: Crawler, Bird, Wingbeater (base Dir), Hopper, Scavenger (private _dir).
