### Key Discovery
- **mgfxc needs Wine on BOTH Linux and macOS** — MonoGame's effect compiler cross-compiles HLSL→GLSL via Wine+fxc. No way around it.
- Shader is compile-time only — once compiled, ships as binary in build output. Recipients don't need Wine.
- Rosetta 2 also needed (Wine is Intel-only, Ethan has Apple Silicon Mac).
