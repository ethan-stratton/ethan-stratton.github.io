### THE BIG QUESTION: Game Hook
Ethan asked what makes Genesis unique — not just another metroidvania with a good story. Acknowledged current design is generic.

**Three hook proposals presented:**
1. **Naming System** — Adam names creatures (Genesis 2:19-20). Observe + scan → name → tame. Wrong name = aggro, right name = ally for settlement. Every encounter = fight or study choice.
2. **Knowledge as Currency** — Understanding replaces XP. Tree of Knowledge literally. Abilities unlock from knowing, not items. Dragon gates with ignorance not power. Flips metroidvania formula.
3. **Ecosystem is the Level** — Rain World inspired. Creatures eat each other, compete, respond to settlement. Building near Grazers removes Crawlers. Build too much → Stalkers.

**Recommended combo: 1 + 3 (Naming + Ecosystem)**
- Pitch: "The metroidvania where understanding your enemies is more powerful than killing them"
- Name creatures to recruit them, use ecology strategically for settlement defense
- Thematically perfect for Genesis narrative
