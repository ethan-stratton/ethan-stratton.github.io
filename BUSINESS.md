## Decision Framework
Each project gets assessed on:
1. **Self-sustaining?** Can it cover its own infra + operational costs?
2. **Demand validated?** Real willingness to pay, not just interest?
3. **Competitive moat?** Why can't someone else do this trivially?
4. **Founder fit?** Can we build and operate it with current resources?
5. **Upside potential?** Any path to breakout, or permanently small?
