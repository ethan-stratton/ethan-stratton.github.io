Little Guy Is Out

I shipped my first game. It's a small, experimental thing — a little creature made of code who discovers words, finds edges, and eventually asks why.

It's browser-playable, free, and takes about 10 minutes. There are four endings depending on how you treat him.

I built it in vanilla JavaScript and HTML5 Canvas. One file. No engine, no framework. Just code and a question.
