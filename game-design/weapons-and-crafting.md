# Weapons & Crafting Design Doc

## Melee Weapons

### Whip
- Disjointed hitbox (reaches beyond player)
- Swappable tips: anchor, meteor hammer style, others TBD
- Cartable (can change tips mid-run?)

### Sword
- Standard melee

### Great Sword
- Slower, heavier hits

### Club
- Blunt damage

### Great Club
- Heavy blunt

### Dagger
- Fast, light
- **Adds combos to vault kick**

## Ammunition & Crafting

### Ammo Types
- Different **arrowheads** (pierce, fire, poison, explode, etc.)
- Different **bullets** (same idea)
- Crafted with **crucible**

### Crafting System
- **NPC assistant** crafts items for you during downtime
- Recipes TBD — need to decide crafting recipes for each weapon/ammo type
- Crucible is the core crafting tool
